About Me

 

 Hi! My name is Eduardo, most known as Teddy, I'm from Portugal and I'm currently 23 years old.

I've studied Multimidia and Graphics Design in Caldas da Rainha situated in Leiria, Portugal.

This journey into the 3D world started in 2012 with minecraft animations and since then I fell in love with 3D modelling and I spent hours and hours getting my work to the next level, always searching for new features and new things to add to my animations!

 Since I discovered AI I found my style by mixing 3D animation with Artificial Intelligence

and I think that really took me to another level.

Nowdays I tend to push myself for inovative ways to use AI with 3D and my work is proof of that, 100% of my animations have some Artificial Intelligence input either in the creative process or in post production.
